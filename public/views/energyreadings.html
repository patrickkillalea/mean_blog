Where appropriate and necessary, messages from your departed loved ones, spirit guides and angels are also included – but if you're curious about a particular person or being coming through, we can work together to tap into their energy so that they may provide a message for you through me


During a reading, I am simply interpreting energy and will provide you with the best translation of what I feel the spirit world is telling me, and what your soul needs for you to hear – but remember that, ultimately, only YOU know what is best for you. I'm simply here to help and to provide comfort, guidance and validation
